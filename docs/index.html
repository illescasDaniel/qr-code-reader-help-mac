  • Basics

  • How to scan QR codes

    Just press the "Take picture" button, then click the red button to take a picture. Now you click on "use photo". A popup will appear with the scanned barcode text or it will open an appropiate window automatically (the browser if is a web link, a new contact popup if is a contact, etc).

    TIP: you can change some of these settings in the preferences window.

  • How to see the codes I scanned

    Open the Recents tab and you will find all your codes there. You can tap on one to view the QR code.

  • How to generate barcodes

    The third tab of the app is what you're looking for. You can generate barcodes from a location, a contact, emails, sms's, etc.
